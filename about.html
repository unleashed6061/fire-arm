About Our Business

Firearm Pro is a licensed firearms business based in Tanzania, focused on providing reliable, high-quality firearms and tactical equipment to law-abiding citizens, security firms, and government agencies. We are committed to promoting responsible gun ownership and safety.

Our core values include:

  • Safety First – We prioritize safe handling and usage of all firearms, with mandatory training sessions for every buyer.
  • Customer Trust – Every product is tested and sourced from internationally recognized manufacturers.
  • Legal Compliance – We work in accordance with national and international laws and regulations.

We specialize in:

  • Handguns and pistols for personal protection
  • Hunting and sporting rifles
  • Ammunition and accessories
  • Protective gear and tactical clothing
  • Training and certification programs

Firearm Pro has earned a reputation for excellence, professionalism, and service. Whether you're a first-time buyer or a licensed security company, we offer personalized guidance and product recommendations to meet your needs.
